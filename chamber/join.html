Applicant Information
NP Membership (Free)
Our NP Membership is designed for nonprofit organizations seeking to connect, collaborate, and grow. This membership level provides essential resources and networking opportunities without any fees.
  • Access to online community forums
  • Monthly newsletter with updates and resources
  • Invitations to free webinars and events
  • Basic listing in the member directory
Bronze Membership
The Bronze Membership offers additional benefits for nonprofits looking to enhance their impact and visibility. This level includes all NP Membership features, plus exclusive perks.
  • All NP Membership benefits
  • Access to a library of resources, including templates and guides
  • Discounts on workshops and training sessions
  • Priority support from our member services team
  • Enhanced listing in the member directory with logo and description
Silver Membership
Silver Membership is tailored for nonprofits ready to take their engagement and effectiveness to the next level. It includes all Bronze Membership benefits, plus further enhancements to support your mission.
  • All Bronze Membership benefits
  • Access to a mentorship program with industry experts
  • Free admission to select events and conferences
  • Opportunities to participate in collaborative projects and grants
  • Featured spot in the member directory and newsletter
  • One free annual consultation with our strategic advisor team
Gold Membership
The Gold Membership is our premier level, offering the most comprehensive suite of benefits for nonprofits committed to maximizing their influence and reach. It includes all Silver Membership benefits, plus exclusive advantages.
  • All Silver Membership benefits
  • Dedicated account manager for personalized support
  • Unlimited access to all workshops, training sessions, and webinars
  • Exclusive invitations to high-profile events and networking opportunities
  • Prominent feature in all communications and promotional materials
  • Access to advanced data analytics tools for impact measurement
  • Priority consideration for spotlight features and case studies
