Design Priciples

Rule of Thirds

HubSpot

hubspot.com

In this page, you can see that they're using the rule of thirds since some of the most important information is found in the corner of the page. We can see that the title is in the upper left corner, and the chart moves towards the right-hand side, which can cause the user to spend more time on this page.

White Space

Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints

https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/?lang=eng

The church's website does a good job at using whitespace, and in this case. the space is literally White, to order the page to make it more readable and understandable

Fitt's Law

Audible

audible.com

The website of audible makes good use of its law by enlarging certain aspects of the screen to make it easier for the user to know what steps to take next.
